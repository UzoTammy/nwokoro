Chores User's Guide

Register

You must be registered to use this app, either as a worker or supervisor. The app's admin is responsible to aid you in registration by providing you with a token. Register here

Sign In or Login

When you sign in as a worker, you will have access to

  • View jobs
  • View the job register - A list of jobs routinely assigned to a user
  • View assigned job(s)
  • View your profile
  • Initiate job
  • Complete task

When you sign in as a supervisor, you will have access to

  • Do all the worker can do as listed above
  • Create and assign jobs
  • Grade the work done
  • View your profile and that of the workers

Job Register

These are already existing jobs that are usually done routinely. Jobs are scheduled from the job register.
