About me

Greetings!

I am Uzo Tammy Nwokoro, a proud alumnus of the Federal University of Technology in Nigeria, where I embarked on my academic journey in Chemical Engineering. My career began with a flourish, as I gained valuable project management experience at Shell Nigeria Gas Ltd, just after graduation. This one-year stint was not just a job; it was a foundation that set the tone for my diverse career path.

Following my time at Shell, I ventured into the distribution sector. Here, I didn't just adapt; I transformed the company. My efforts in computerizing operations streamlined processes, marking a significant leap in efficiency and effectiveness. This achievement wasn't the peak of my journey; it was a stepping stone, leading me to the role of Business Operations Manager. For three years, I steered the company with a blend of technical acumen and strategic foresight, continually driving growth and innovation.

The entrepreneurial spirit in me then took the helm, leading to the founding of my own business. Specializing in the investment and redistribution of alcoholic beverages, I navigated the complex landscape of this industry with skill and determination. This venture wasn't just a business; it was a testament to my ability to identify opportunities and capitalize on them.

Now, as I pivot towards Information Technology, my true passion, I am excited about the endless possibilities this field offers. My journey is a tapestry of diverse experiences, each thread woven with dedication, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. I stand ready to dive into the IT realm, bringing with me a wealth of knowledge and a unique perspective shaped by my varied career path.

Give me a chance as I embark on this new chapter, where the fusion of technology and business acumen promises to create innovative solutions and transformative experiences.
