About Me

Greetings!

I am Uzo Tammy Nwokoro, a graduate of the Federal University of Technology in Nigeria, as a Chemical Engineer. My career began with a flourish, as I gained valuable project management experience at Shell Nigeria Gas Ltd, just after graduation. This one-year stint was not just a job; it was a foundation that set the tone for my diverse career path. Through my time in the university, I developed my skills in programming.

Following my time at Shell, I ventured into the distribution sector. Here, I didn't just adapt; I transformed the company. Led it's computerized operations and streamlined processes, marking a significant leap in efficiency and effectiveness. This achievement landed me the role of Business Operations Manager. For three years, I steered the company with a blend of technical acumen and strategic foresight, continually driving growth and innovation.

The entrepreneurial spirit in me then took the helm, leading to the founding of my own business. Specializing in the investment and redistribution of alcoholic beverages, I navigated the complex landscape of this industry with skill and determination. This venture wasn't just a business; it was a testament to my ability to identify opportunities and capitalize on them.

Now, as I pivot towards Information Technology, my true passion, I am excited about the endless possibilities this field offers. My journey is a tapestry of diverse experiences, each thread woven with dedication, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. I stand ready to dive into the IT realm, bringing with me a wealth of knowledge and a unique perspective shaped by my varied career path.

When I take up a job, I am only inclined to the business case. Where the fusion of technology and business acumen promises to create innovative solutions. I have deepened my knowledge in Project Management through a Google career opportunity.

Skills

  • Sales: I have been involved in sales for more than a decade. As I have coordinated, supervised and directed sales spanning more than 100 millions dollars over this period.
  • Customer Relations: Being successful in sales involves positive customer relations. The constant interactions with customers and their issues sharpened me.
  • Team Collaboration: I have interpersonal and team skills. Constantly, persuading a team to stay positive with customers while retaining company's policies and procedures is about the most important duty.
  • Leadership: While in the university, I was elected President of the department. I also had the opportunity to lead various teams throughout my career in the distribution business.
  • Resolving Problems: Business can't be successful except you are able to solve problems. To develop a software, it is problem solving and to execute a project is also.
